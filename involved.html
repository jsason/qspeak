About Us

QSpeak is a national program dedicated to fostering a better connected and informed community of LGBTQ+ high school students in Canada.

Participants will fill out a survey designed to match them with 3-4 other students, forming groups each assigned a QSpeak Ambassador (a university student volunteer).

Each week throughout the program will be dedicated to a specific theme , which will be announced via email and social media along with a related discussion question. 

The ambassador will then initiate conversations via instant messaging, and a weekly 15-30 minute text, voice, or video meeting which will include further discussion questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Do I Join?

Fill out the quick matching survey and that’s it!​ After we’ve matched you with a group, a QSpeak Ambassador will get in contact with you with more information on how to meet your group members!

How Does Group-Making Work?

With the information from your matching survey, we’ll match you with your group based on the following information:

  • Similarity in age 
  • Your preference to be matched with someone geographically near or far from you
  • Your preferred communication platforms

The last question of the matching survey gives you an opportunity to make any special requests for your matches.

What Will I Be Doing?

Each week will be based off a specific theme about being LGBTQ+. For example: coming out, representation in the media, and queer history. Each week will also have a central question designed to get you and your group thinking more about what it means to be a member of the LGBTQ+ community.​ The QSpeak Ambassador in your group will then find a time when all group members are free, and arrange a platform where you can all meet (Zoom, iMessage, etc..). During this time, you and your group will be having a  discussion about the theme, guided by the QSpeak Ambassador. Discussions should be no longer than 30 minutes per week with an average of around 15 minutes. ​Your group will also have the opportunity during meetings to participate in activities for the chance to win a $5 Starbucks gift card each! As well, your responses will be compiled into forms of art each week to visually represent our QSpeak communication.

Rules

Think before you QSpeak! For a full explanation of the rules, see our terms and conditions.

1

No judgement, discrimination, abuse, bullying, harassment, or disrespect of any form.

2

Should you ever be placed in an uncomfortable situation by your buddy, contact us at @q.speak (Instagram) or our email

3

If you need immediate support, consult hotlines

4

If you feel that your safety or that of any others is endangered, and you cannot deal with it on your own, please call 911.

5

Unless given written permission (via our email or Instagram), you may not replicate the content used in all QSpeak programs without reference to the original source.
